Our Heritage

A Legacy of Culinary Excellence

Founded in 2010, NOUGI represents the perfect fusion of French pastry artistry and Lebanese hospitality. Our founder, trained in the finest pâtisseries of Paris, brought these refined techniques to the heart of Beirut.

Every morning, our master pastry chefs create extraordinary delicacies using only the finest imported ingredients, ensuring each bite delivers an unforgettable experience.
