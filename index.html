Our Heritage

A Legacy of Culinary Excellence

Founded in 2010, NOUGI represents the perfect fusion of French pastry artistry and Lebanese hospitality. Our founder trained under master pâtissiers at Paris's most prestigious establishments before bringing these refined techniques to Beirut.

Chef Michel Habib, Founder

Recognized by Gault & Millau as Beirut's most authentic French-Lebanese patisserie since 2018

Each morning at 4 AM, our team begins crafting with only the finest ingredients:

  • French AOP butter from Normandy
  • Belgian Callebaut chocolate
  • Organic Lebanese pistachios
  • Madagascar vanilla beans
